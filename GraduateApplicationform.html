Graduate Requirements

At University Of Ghana, we have specific requirements that students must fulfill in order to graduate from their respective programs. The following are the general guidelines:

Academic Requirements

Students must successfully complete the required number of credits for their chosen program. This includes both core and elective courses.

GPA (Grade Point Average)

A minimum GPA of 3.5 is required for graduation. The exact GPA requirement may vary by program, so it's essential for students to check the specific requirements for their Program.

Program-Specific Requirements

Some programs have specific requirements that students must meet. This may include internships, capstone projects, or other hands-on experiences relevant to the Academic Program

Elective Courses

Students may have the option to choose elective courses within certain parameters. The fulfillment of elective requirements depends on the program and is often designed to allow students to explore areas of interest.

Residency Requirements

Certain programs may have residency requirements, a minimum of 150 credits must be completed at our institution for graduation.

Application for Graduation

Students must submit a formal application for graduation by a specified deadline. This initiates the graduation process and ensures that all requirements are met.

It's important for students to consult with their academic advisors regularly to ensure they are on track to meet these requirements and to address any questions or concerns they may have.

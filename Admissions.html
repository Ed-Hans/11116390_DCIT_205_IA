Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions page of the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana. We are excited about your interest in joining our academic community.

Programs Offered

The Computer Science Department offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in the field of computer science.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

    • Apply for our Undergraduate on our UndergraduateApplication Portal

  • Master of Science in Computer Science
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science

    • Apply for our Graduate on our GraduateApplication Portal

Admission Requirements

Admission requirements may vary depending on the program you are applying for. Please review the specific admission requirements for our Undergraduate and Graduate programs.

How to Apply

Follow the steps below to apply for admission to the Computer Science Department:

  1. Visit the university's official admissions portal.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Submit all required documents, including transcripts and recommendation letters.
  4. Pay the application fee.
  5. Track your application status through the online portal.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance with the admissions process, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our admissions team is here to help you!

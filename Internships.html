About Our Internship Program

Our internship program provides students with a valuable opportunity to gain hands-on experience in their field of study. The program is designed to complement academic coursework and prepare students for success in their future careers.

Eligibility Criteria

Our internship program is open to undergraduate and graduate students of the department. Students must meet the following requirements:

  • Be currently enrolled in a Program at our departement program
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Be in level 200 and above

Application Process

To apply for the internship program, follow these steps:

  1. Review the eligibility criteria to ensure you meet the requirements.
  2. Prepare your resume and a cover letter expressing your interest in the program.
  3. Complete the online application form on our website.
  4. Submit any additional required documents, such as transcripts and motivation letter.
  5. Attend an interview.
  6. Selected candidates will be notified via email.

Internship Opportunities

Our internship program offers opportunities in various fields, including:

  1. Software development
  2. Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  3. Web Development
  4. Cybersecurity
  5. Database Admission
  6. Data Science
  7. Hardware Engineering

    8. Interns will work on meaningful projects, collaborate with experienced professionals, and gain insights into the industry.

Benefits of Our Internship Program

  • Hands-on experience in a real-world setting
  • Networking opportunities with professionals in the field
  • Mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals
  • Enhanced skills and knowledge relevant to your major
  • Possibility of earning academic credit

For more information or inquiries about our internship program, please email us at CS-department@dep.ug.edu.gh.

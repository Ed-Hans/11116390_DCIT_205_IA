Undergraduate Admission Requirements

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for undergraduate admission, applicants must meet the following criteria:
  • Completed secondary education or equivalent.
  • A minimum of B2 in Elective Mathematics (WASSCE) is required.
  • The cutoff grade for Computer Science is 10.
  • The cutoff point for Information Technology is 15 with a minimun of B3 in core mathematics.
  • English language proficiency test scores international appilcants is 7.5 on the IELTS and 750 on the SAT.

Application Process

The application process for undergraduate admission includes the following steps:
  1. Submit an online application form
  2. Provide official transcripts from all previous academic institutions
  3. Submit letters of recommendation (if required)
  4. Write a personal statement or essay (if required)
  5. Pay the application fee

Important Dates

Make sure to adhere to the following important dates and deadlines:
  • Application deadline: 20th November,2023.
  • Notification of admission decisions: 20th December,2023.
  • Acceptance Deadline: 5th January, 2024.
  • Accommodation Application Deadline: 12th January, 2024.
  • School resumes on the:15th of January, 2024.
